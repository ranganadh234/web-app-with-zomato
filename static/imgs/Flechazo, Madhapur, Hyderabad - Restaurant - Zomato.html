        Rated
          Being new to a city, Zomato is huge rescue when you have to decide on a restaurant to eat at.
        Flechazo, with a rating like this, was in my radar since quite sometime and when I had to catch up with a friend, I chose this place without a second thought.

        We were there on a Monday and from what I learnt it's an out-and-out buffet place on Mondays. Our bad as we were looking to eat light and converse more.

        Anyway, moving on..Their ambiance looked like a true buffet restaurant - not sure if that's how it is everyday. It was pretty crowded for a Monday afternoon. People everywhere, noise everywhere.

        Coming to the food.. They have a summer special buffet going on where they serve a few dishes that are purely mango based.
        - The welcome drink was a mango based one: decent.
        - Soup: Tried Aam Shorba, did not like it.
        - The starters: Like that in a buffet, there were too many. But what blew me over was the Turkish roll. It was amazing. From mango special they had mango chilli prawns - in the first serving I could barely taste anything mango so for the second serving they increased the mango jelly (I guess), it was okay. Rest of the starters were quite good.
        - The live counters: did not try them but they looked pretty inviting and they had too much crowd.
        - The main course: I went ahead with a veg platter - liked nothing. Good was mediocre to say the least. Friend had chicken biryani and he didn't like it either.
        - Desserts: Gulab jamun and Pineapple jalebi with Kesari were really good. Rest ain't worth mentioning.

        Not sure why they have a 4.8 rating while our experience was a 3 at best.
        The service is really good tho. The experience did not feel worth a 610 bucks I paid for.
